Research Interests

I am broadly interested in Computer Science and Mathematics, particularly in the intersection of probabilistic models, spectral graph theory, and optimization. My recent work has explored topics such as fraud detection in random walks and optimization in disordered hyper-uniform materials. I am especially interested in how theoretical advancements can be applied to real-world computational challenges.

I extend my gratitude to my research advisors, Professor Claire Gmachl and Professor Pedro Paredes, for their invaluable guidance and mentorship.
